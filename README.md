  ## Business Insights
  ### 1. Poor Data Quality Issues
  - Findings: Only 1.84 % of rows from raw data is complete to be included in analysis as there are missing, inaccurate & illogical values.
  - Implications: Inconsistent & inaccurate data could affect business decision strategies,
    & data entry workflow may have potential difficulties.
  - Recommendations: Conduct survey with staffs to improve data entry workflow, include automatic formulas for more complex formulas.
 
  ### 2. Profitability Differ Throughout Product Lines & Customers
  - Findings: 1) Soccer & Running products have better profitability while Training & Lifestyle have subpar profitability.
              2) Products for men generate most revenues but lesser profit whereas kids products are more profitable but
                 generate lesser revenues.
  - Implications: Opportunity to maximise & improve sales efficiency for different products.
  - Recommendations: Evaluate success factors for high performing products to be applied towards other products
    & devise better strategies to maximise high performing product's performance.

 ### 3. Moderate Discounts Shows Better Sales Efficiency
  - Findings: 20-40% category yields the best sales efficiency.
  - Implications: More discounts does not mean better sales efficiency.
  - Recommendations: Promotion & discount strategies should be devised carefully during campaigns.

### 4. Retail Channel Performs Slightly Better in Sales
  - Findings: Retail stores have slightly better sales & revenue contributions currently.
  - Implications: Customers have slight preferences for physical stores, online store visibility can be improved.
  - Recommendations: Optimise inventory planning, stock availability & customer experience for retail & design
    better online marketing strategies.
